Our Services

Exporting Nigerian culture with speed, care, and reliability.

Why Choose AfriShip?

We help diaspora families stay connected to their roots by exporting carefully sourced, culturally relevant items from Nigeria. Our team handles sourcing, packaging, shipping, and compliance with attention to detail and care.

What We Offer

Sourcing & Packaging

We go to the open markets for you—picking fresh garri, herbs, spices, cloth, and dried proteins. Every item is hygienically packaged and labeled to meet shipping standards.

Export & Shipping

We partner with logistics and airport services to ship your goods to the UK, US, and Canada—delivered in as little as 5–7 days. You get tracking and regular updates.

Labeling & Compliance

Each shipment is labeled according to international guidelines, ensuring smooth clearance at customs and delivery to your doorstep without stress.

Diaspora Gifting

Send surprise cultural gift boxes to loved ones abroad. Whether for birthdays, celebrations, or just because—we pack love and culture into every box.

How It Works

  1. Step 1: Contact us via WhatsApp or our contact form
  2. Step 2: Tell us what items you need and where it's going
  3. Step 3: We source, pack, label, and ship within 24–72 hours
  4. Step 4: Your goods arrive within 5–7 days (UK), 7–10 days (US/Canada)
